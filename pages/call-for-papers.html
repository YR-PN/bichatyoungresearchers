Call for Papers

We invite submissions of original research from Master's interns, doctoral and post-doctoral fellows, engineers from partner labs:

  • CRI (UMR 1149)
  • IAME (UMR 1137)
  • LVTS (UMRS 1148)

Presentation Formats

Oral Communications

Present your research and receive feedback.

Poster Sessions

Showcase your work and most beautiful figures.

'Pitch Your Project' (PYP) Session

180 seconds to present your research project.

Submission Guidelines

Abstract Length: 250-500 words

Format: Plain text (see form)

Submission Deadline: September 1, 2026, 23:59 (Paris time)

Your submission should include:

  • Title of the paper
  • Author names and affiliations
  • Introduction and motivation
  • Methods or approach
  • Key findings or expected results
  • Significance and impact
  • Number of words in main text
  • Up to 5 relevant keywords
