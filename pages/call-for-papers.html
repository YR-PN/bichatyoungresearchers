Scope

We invite submissions of original research from Master's interns, doctoral and post-doctoral fellows, engineers from partner labs:

  • Research Track I: [Your Topic]
  • Research Track II: [Your Topic]

Submission Guidelines

Abstract Length: 250-500 words

Format: Plain text or PDF

Submission Deadline: September 10, 2026, 23:59 (Paris)

Your abstract should include:

  • Title of the paper
  • Author names and affiliations
  • Background and motivation
  • Methods or approach
  • Key findings or expected results
  • Significance and impact
  • Up to 5 relevant keywords
