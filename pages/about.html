About the Conference

Welcome

The Bichat Young Researchers Association brings together young researchers from Inserm labs (CRI, IAME, LVTS, TETECOU), to foster good working conditions and relations among peers. Year-round, we organize periodical events (such as afterwork mixers), and hold a yearly conference that allows you to present your research, whatever its advancement (project, stub, or published).

For 5 years, this conference has grown into platform for knowledge exchange, networking, and collaboration among our local research community.

Bichat Young Researchers Day: Who Should Attend?

Each year, BYRD welcomes:

  • Masters students and other interns,
  • PhD students and postdoctoral researchers,
  • Research engineers and technicians,
  • University researchers and faculty (it's bring your PI day!),
  • Our generous institutional and industrial sponsors.

Accessibility & Inclusivity

We are committed to making SciConf 2025 inclusive and accessible to all. The conference venue is wheelchair accessible, and we provide:

  • Accessible parking and public transportation information
  • Real-time captioning for all plenary sessions
  • Accessible restroom facilities
  • Dietary accommodations and allergen information
  • Quiet spaces for those who need a break
  • Childcare services (upon request, in advance)

If you have specific accessibility needs, please contact us at accessibility@sciconf2025.org
