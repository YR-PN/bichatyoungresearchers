About us

The association

The Bichat Young Researchers Association brings together young researchers from Inserm labs (CRI, IAME, LVTS, Oral Health), to foster good working conditions and relations among early-career peers.
Year-round, we organize periodical events (such as afterwork mixers), and hold a yearly conference that allows you to present your research, whatever its advancement (project, stub, or published). For 5 years, this conference has grown into platform for knowledge exchange, networking, and collaboration among our local research community.
