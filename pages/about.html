About Bichat Young Researchers

The association

The Bichat Young Researchers Association brings together young researchers from Inserm labs (CRI, IAME, LVTS, Oral Health), to foster good working conditions and relations among early-career peers.
Year-round, we organize periodical events (such as afterwork mixers), and hold a yearly conference that allows you to present your research, whatever its advancement (project, stub, or published).

For 5 years, this conference has grown into platform for knowledge exchange, networking, and collaboration among our local research community.

Bichat Young Researchers Day: Who Should Attend?

Each year, BYRD welcomes:

  • Masters students and other interns,
  • PhD students and postdoctoral researchers,
  • Research engineers and technicians,
  • University researchers and faculty (it's bring your PI day!),
  • Our generous institutional and industrial sponsors.
