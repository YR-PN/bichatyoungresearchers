Call for Papers

Scope

We invite submissions of original research in the following areas:

  • Research Track I: [Your Topic]
  • Research Track II: [Your Topic]
  • Research Track III: [Your Topic]
  • Applications & Case Studies
  • Emerging Topics & Position Papers

Submission Guidelines

Abstract Length: 250-500 words

Format: Plain text or PDF

Submission Deadline: June 15, 2025, 23:59 UTC

Your abstract should include:

  • Title of the paper
  • Author names and affiliations
  • Background and motivation
  • Methods or approach
  • Key findings or expected results
  • Significance and impact
  • Up to 5 relevant keywords

Important Dates

Abstract Submission Opens April 1, 2025
Submission Deadline June 15, 2025
Peer Review Period June 16 - July 30, 2025
Notification of Acceptance July 31, 2025
Camera-Ready Deadline August 30, 2025
Conference Dates October 1-3, 2025

Peer Review

All submissions will be reviewed by at least two members of the program committee. Reviews will be double-blind—authors should ensure their submission does not reveal their identity.

Accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings.
